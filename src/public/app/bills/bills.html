Welcome! Use #FLVote to harness Twitter to influence Bills in the Florida Legislature.

Vote Yes or No in just 3 easy steps:

  1. Pick the Bill
  2. Vote #YES or #NO to create your tweet
  3. Share it on Twitter

Promote your views or add your favorite hashtag by customizing your tweet before sharing. If someone ReTweets yours, then that’s another vote for your side.

We don’t store any of your info. The conversation happens out on the Twitterverse.
